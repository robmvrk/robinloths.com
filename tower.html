The Investment Case

Why Paraguay?

A unique convergence of macroeconomic stability, undervalued assets, and investor-friendly policy makes Asunción one of the smartest real estate plays in the world right now.

★

Investment-Grade Rating

Paraguay received a Baa3 investment-grade rating from Moody's in July 2024, signaling macroeconomic stability and sound fiscal policy since 2002. This upgrade accelerates growth and real estate upside.

▲

Undervalued Market

Apartments in Asunción trade at 41% below the average of other Latin American capitals — offering investors significant capital appreciation potential as the market corrects.

◆

0% Foreign Income Tax

Paraguay's territorial tax system means zero tax on foreign-sourced income. Maximum 10% tax rate overall, with just 5% on rental income. A true investor's haven.

✈

#1 Tourism Destination

Paraguay ranked #1 worldwide in Q1 2025 UN Tourism Report with 53% growth in international arrivals — the fastest growing destination on Earth.

⏱

Extreme Liquidity

Apartments sell in 40–50 days on average and rent within 20–35 days. Strong demand and high market liquidity reduce your risk.

⚐

Easy Residency

Minimal requirements (birth certificate + criminal record). No minimum stay required to maintain residency. Low cost of living, warm climate, and vibrant modern city life.
